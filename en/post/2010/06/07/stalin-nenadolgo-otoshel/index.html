Stalin departed for a short while

Last updated on May 17, 2020 1 min read blog

Roosevelt and Churchill are sitting. The monument is called “Allies”, but more appropriate, “Stalin departed for a short while.”

This bronze, seated Churchill and Roosevelt statue called “Allies” was sculpted by Lawrence Holofcener and unveiled in 1995 by Princess Margaret. Located in downtown London in the pedestrian area in Old Bond Street, visitors to the statue frequently have their photograph taken seated between the two famous personages.
